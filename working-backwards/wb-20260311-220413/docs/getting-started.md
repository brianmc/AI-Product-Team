# Getting Started with High Flyer

High Flyer uses your existing Xfinite credentials — there is no separate account to create. If you can log in to your Xfinite account, you can use High Flyer.

---

## Before your first booking

You need:

- An active Xfinite affluent card
- Your Xfinite username and password
- Access to [highflyer.xfinite.com](https://highflyer.xfinite.com) from any modern web browser

High Flyer works on desktop, tablet, and mobile. No app download is required.

---

## Signing in

1. Go to [highflyer.xfinite.com](https://highflyer.xfinite.com).
2. Select **Sign In with Xfinite**.
3. Enter your Xfinite username and password.
4. `[TBD — pre-launch]` Additional verification (such as multi-factor authentication) may be required before your first booking is confirmed. Details will be published at launch.
5. Once signed in, you land on the **City Selection** screen.

If you have forgotten your Xfinite credentials, use the password reset flow at [xfinite.com/account](https://xfinite.com/account). High Flyer does not manage passwords separately.

---

## Confirming your card eligibility

High Flyer is available to all Xfinite affluent cardholders. When you sign in, the application verifies your card status automatically. If your card is active and eligible, you proceed directly to browsing.

If you see a message indicating your card is not eligible, contact Xfinite cardholder support using the number on the back of your card.

---

## Your first time in the app

After signing in, you will see three sections:

| Section | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Select a City** | Cities where High Flyer venues are currently available |
| **Tonight / Tomorrow** | A toggle to switch between same-day and next-day availability |
| **Your Reservations** | Any upcoming or past bookings on your account |

To make your first booking, select a city, choose a venue, and confirm your reservation. The full booking flow is covered in [Booking a Table](booking-a-table.md).
