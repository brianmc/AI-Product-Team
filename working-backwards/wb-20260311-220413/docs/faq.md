# Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Access and eligibility

**Who can use High Flyer?**

High Flyer is available to all active Xfinite affluent cardholders. Access is included with your card — no application, no upgrade, and no points redemption required.

**How do I access High Flyer?**

Visit [highflyer.xfinite.com](https://highflyer.xfinite.com) and sign in with your existing Xfinite credentials. There is no separate account or app to download.

## Reservations and guarantees

**The press release says reservations are "guaranteed." What does that mean exactly?**

A confirmed High Flyer reservation means a table is held for you at the venue. `[TBD — pre-launch]` The precise scope of the guarantee — including what Xfinite will do if a venue is unable to honor a confirmed booking — will be defined in the cardholder terms at launch. The intent is that you are never left without recourse.

**What do I do if I arrive and the venue says they have no record of my booking?**

Show your booking confirmation and ask to speak with a manager. If the venue is still unable to seat you, contact High Flyer support. `[TBD — pre-launch]` Same-night recovery options and the support contact will be published at launch. For immediate assistance, contact Xfinite cardholder support using the number on the back of your card.

**Can I cancel a reservation?**

Yes. Go to **Your Reservations** in the app and select **Cancel Reservation**. Please cancel as early as possible — High Flyer venues hold tables specifically for your booking, and late cancellations or no-shows affect the venue's ability to seat their regulars.

`[TBD — pre-launch]` The cancellation deadline, any applicable fees for late cancellation or no-show, and whether repeated no-shows affect access will be published in the cardholder terms at launch.

**How does the venue know I'm an Xfinite cardholder when I arrive?**

## Privacy and security

**What data does High Flyer collect about me?**

High Flyer records your bookings: which city, which venue, which date, and your party size. See [Privacy and Data](privacy-and-data.md) for a full description.

**What does the venue receive about me?**

Venues receive the information needed to seat you: your name, party size, and reservation date. `[TBD — pre-launch]` Whether venues receive any additional cardholder information, and the contractual limits on how they use it, will be disclosed in the cardholder privacy notice at launch.

**Does Xfinite use my High Flyer booking history for marketing or credit decisions?**

`[TBD — pre-launch]` Xfinite's policy on internal use of High Flyer booking data will be published in the updated cardholder privacy notice at launch.

**Someone else has access to my Xfinite credentials. Can they book through High Flyer?**

A booking made using your credentials is associated with your cardholder record. Do not share your login details. If your Xfinite account has been compromised, contact Xfinite cardholder support immediately using the number on the back of your card.
