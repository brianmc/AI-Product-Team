# Troubleshooting

Most High Flyer bookings proceed without issue. When something goes wrong, the steps below will resolve the most common situations.

---

## Venue not honoring a booking

### Before your visit: you receive a cancellation notice

If High Flyer or the venue notifies you that your reservation cannot be honored — due to an unexpected closure, a private event, or another reason — here is what to do:

1. Return to the app and check current availability for the same city and evening. Another venue may have open slots.
2. `[TBD — pre-launch]` High Flyer's policy on re-routing or compensating cardholders when a reservation is cancelled before arrival will be published at launch.

### At the door: the venue has no record of your booking or cannot seat you

This should not happen. If it does:

1. Show the venue your booking confirmation (on screen or printed) and give them your name and the phrase "Xfinite High Flyer benefit."
2. Ask to speak with a manager. High Flyer reservations are held under your name and should be visible to venue management even if front-of-house staff are not familiar with the program.
3. If the venue is still unable or unwilling to seat you, do not escalate on-site. Leave the venue and contact High Flyer support.

`[TBD — pre-launch]` The support contact number and same-night recovery process (alternative venue, compensation) will be published at launch. Xfinite cardholder support is available via the number on the back of your card as an interim escalation path.

## Sign-in issues

### "Your card is not eligible for High Flyer"

High Flyer is available to all active Xfinite affluent cardholders. If you see an eligibility error:

- Confirm your card account is in good standing by logging in to [xfinite.com/account](https://xfinite.com/account).
- If your account is active but the error persists, contact Xfinite cardholder support. The eligibility check links to your Xfinite account record — support can confirm your card's benefit status.

### Forgotten Xfinite password

Use the password reset flow at [xfinite.com/account](https://xfinite.com/account). High Flyer cannot reset your Xfinite credentials.

### Sign-in loop or page not loading

- Clear your browser cache and try again.
- Try a different browser or device.
- If the issue persists, the application may be experiencing temporary downtime. `[TBD — pre-launch]` A status page will be available at launch.

## App shows no available venues

### No venues listed for a city

On any given evening, available venue slots depend on what is committed for that date. If no venues appear:

- Try the **Tomorrow** toggle — availability for next-day bookings may differ.
- Try a different city if you have flexibility.
- Check back later in the evening — venue availability occasionally updates.

This is not a system error. High Flyer's curated list is small by design, and some evenings a city may have limited or no open slots.

### A city I expected to see is not in the list

`[TBD — pre-launch]` The confirmed launch city list will be published at launch. Cities will be added over time. If your destination is not listed, High Flyer does not yet have committed venue availability there.

## Booking confirmation not received

If you completed the booking flow but did not receive a confirmation email:

1. Check your spam or junk folder for an email from Xfinite High Flyer.
2. Sign in to High Flyer and check **Your Reservations** — if the booking is listed there as confirmed, the reservation is valid regardless of whether the email arrived.
3. If the booking does not appear in Your Reservations either, the booking was not completed. Attempt to book again.

---

## Cancellation not processed

If you cancelled a reservation but it still appears as upcoming in Your Reservations:

1. Refresh the page or sign out and back in.
2. If the reservation still appears as active after a few minutes, contact High Flyer support `[TBD — pre-launch]` via the in-app support channel or Xfinite cardholder support.

---

## Something else went wrong

For issues not covered here, contact Xfinite cardholder support using the number on the back of your card. Have your booking confirmation reference available if your issue relates to a specific reservation.
