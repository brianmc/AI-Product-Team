# Your Reservations

All confirmed High Flyer bookings appear under **Your Reservations** in the application. This section covers how to view, manage, and cancel a reservation, and what to bring when you arrive.

---

## Viewing your reservations

Sign in to [highflyer.xfinite.com](https://highflyer.xfinite.com) and select **Your Reservations** from the home screen. You will see:

- **Upcoming reservations** — bookings confirmed for tonight or a future date
- **Past reservations** — your booking history

Each reservation entry shows the venue name, city, date, party size, and confirmation status.

---

## Your confirmation artifact

After confirming a booking, you receive:

- An on-screen confirmation
- A confirmation email to your Xfinite account email address
- `[TBD — pre-launch]` A booking artifact (such as a QR code or alphanumeric booking reference) that you present at the venue door

Keep the confirmation accessible on your phone before you arrive. `[TBD — pre-launch]` The specific format of the confirmation artifact and the verification method used at the door will be confirmed at launch.

---

## What to bring to the venue

- Your confirmation (on screen or printed)
- A valid government-issued ID matching the name on your Xfinite account
- `[TBD — pre-launch]` Whether your physical Xfinite card is required for verification at the door will be confirmed at launch

---

## Cancelling a reservation

If your plans change, cancel as early as possible. High Flyer venues hold tables specifically for your booking — a late cancellation or no-show means a seat goes unfilled that a regular could have had.

`[TBD — pre-launch]` The cancellation policy — including any deadline for fee-free cancellation, what constitutes a no-show, and whether a no-show or repeated late-cancellation behavior affects your access — will be published in the cardholder terms at launch.

**To cancel a reservation:**

1. Sign in to [highflyer.xfinite.com](https://highflyer.xfinite.com).
2. Go to **Your Reservations**.
3. Select the upcoming reservation you want to cancel.
4. Select **Cancel Reservation** and confirm.

You will receive a cancellation confirmation by email.

---

## Modifying a reservation

At this time, High Flyer does not support modification of a confirmed reservation (for example, changing party size after booking). To change your booking, cancel the existing reservation and create a new one — subject to availability.

`[TBD — pre-launch]` In-app modification functionality will be evaluated for a future release.

---

## If your reservation shows an unexpected status

If a confirmed reservation disappears from Your Reservations, shows as cancelled when you did not cancel it, or you receive a cancellation notice from High Flyer, see [Troubleshooting](troubleshooting.md) for next steps.
