# High Flyer — Cardholder Guide

High Flyer gives Xfinite affluent cardholders a reserved seat at the most sought-after hidden bars in cities they've never called home.

---

## What High Flyer does

When you travel, your social capital doesn't. The speakeasy you'd walk straight into in your own city — the one with no sign on the door and no OpenTable listing — is effectively invisible when you're somewhere new. High Flyer fixes that.

The application presents a curated, city-specific selection of exclusive and hard-to-access bars: the kind of venues that matter to a well-connected urban traveler. You choose a city, pick a venue, and confirm a table for tonight or tomorrow night. No calling ahead. No name-dropping. The access is built into your Xfinite card.

**High Flyer is available at [highflyer.xfinite.com](https://highflyer.xfinite.com).**

---

## Who High Flyer is for

High Flyer is a benefit available to all Xfinite affluent cardholders. You do not need to apply, upgrade, or spend points to use it — access is included with your card.
