# Privacy and Data

High Flyer is a benefit tied to your Xfinite account. When you use it, the application collects data about your bookings. This page explains what is collected, how it is used, and what control you have over it.

---

## What High Flyer collects

Every booking you make through High Flyer generates a record that includes:

- The city selected
- The venue booked
- The date and time of the reservation
- Your party size
- Whether the booking was honored, cancelled, or resulted in a no-show

Over time, this constitutes a record of your travel patterns, destination preferences, and nightlife habits.

## What venues receive

When you make a High Flyer reservation, the venue receives the information necessary to seat you:

- Your name
- Your party size
- Your reservation date and arrival window

## Account security

Your High Flyer access is secured by your Xfinite credentials. To protect your account:

- Use a strong, unique password for your Xfinite account.
- Do not share your login credentials with anyone. A booking made through your account using your credentials is associated with your cardholder record.
- `[TBD — pre-launch]` Multi-factor authentication requirements for High Flyer bookings will be confirmed at launch.

If you believe your Xfinite account has been compromised, contact Xfinite cardholder support immediately using the number on the back of your card.
