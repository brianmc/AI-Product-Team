# Booking a Table

High Flyer is built for short-notice, high-confidence reservations. Most bookings take under two minutes from opening the app to receiving confirmation.

---

## How to book

### Step 1 — Choose your city

On the home screen, select the city you are in or traveling to. Only cities with currently available High Flyer venues appear in the list.

`[TBD — pre-launch]` The full list of launch cities will be confirmed before the product goes live.

### Step 2 — Set your date

Use the **Tonight / Tomorrow** toggle to indicate when you want to visit. High Flyer is designed for same-day and next-day reservations. Bookings further in advance are `[TBD — pre-launch]`.

### Step 3 — Browse available venues

High Flyer shows you the venues with tables available for your selected city and date. The list is short by design — you are seeing the places that meet the standard, not every bar in the city.

Each venue card shows:

- **Venue name** — displayed as the venue has agreed to present it (some venues list only a partial name or a code name)
- **Neighborhood** — the area of the city where the venue is located
- **Type** — a brief description of the kind of experience (e.g., "cocktail bar," "speakeasy," "rooftop lounge")
- **Available slots** — how many table slots are open for your selected evening
- **Party size range** — the minimum and maximum party size the venue will accommodate through High Flyer

Select any venue to see its full detail page.

### Step 4 — Review the venue detail

The venue detail page shows:

- A short description of the venue and its character
- Any specific requirements the venue has asked High Flyer to communicate (dress, arrival window, etc.)
- `[TBD — pre-launch]` Whether any minimum spend or cover applies at this venue. This will be disclosed on the detail page before you confirm.

Read the venue notes before confirming. Expectations set here are expectations the venue holds you to.

### Step 5 — Select your party size and confirm

Choose your party size and select **Confirm Reservation**. You may be asked to `[TBD — pre-launch]` complete an additional verification step before your booking is finalized.

Once confirmed:

- You receive a booking confirmation on screen and by email to your Xfinite account email address.
- Your reservation appears under **Your Reservations** on the High Flyer home screen.
- `[TBD — pre-launch]` A confirmation artifact (such as a QR code or booking reference) will be provided for use at the venue door.

---

## What the confirmation means

A confirmed High Flyer reservation means a table is held for you at the venue. You do not need to call ahead or present anything beyond your confirmation when you arrive.

`[TBD — pre-launch]` The precise scope of what "confirmed" guarantees — and the recovery path if a venue is unable to honor a booking — is being finalized by the Product and Legal teams and will be published in the cardholder terms at launch.

---

## Costs at the venue

High Flyer access is included with your Xfinite card. There are no fees or points required to use the application.

`[TBD — pre-launch]` Some venues may apply a minimum spend or cover charge to High Flyer reservations. Where this applies, it will be displayed on the venue detail page before you confirm your booking. You will never encounter a minimum spend at the door that was not disclosed in the app.

## Arriving at the venue

- Arrive within the arrival window specified on your confirmation. Venues hold tables for High Flyer bookings, but they are not held indefinitely.
- `[TBD — pre-launch]` Bring your confirmation artifact (QR code, booking reference, or other) and a valid ID matching the name on your Xfinite account. The verification method required at the door will be confirmed at launch.
- If you have questions on arrival, give the venue your name and mention that your reservation was made through the Xfinite High Flyer benefit.

---

## If your booking cannot be honored

If you arrive and the venue is unable to seat you, or if the venue contacts you before your visit to say they cannot fulfill your reservation, see [Troubleshooting — Venue Not Honoring a Booking](troubleshooting.md#venue-not-honoring-a-booking).
